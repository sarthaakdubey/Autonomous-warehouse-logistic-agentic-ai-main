**1. Root Cause Analysis:**

* Delays in Indore warehouse primarily due to distance and transport mode
* Inefficient warehouse operations, slower picking times, and transport mode (Van vs Truck) also contribute to delays

**2. Key Insights:**

* Larger quantities do not necessarily cause delays
* Bhopal warehouse has a faster picking time (18 minutes) compared to Indore (45-50 minutes)
* Trucks experience more delays than vans

**3. Industry Best Practices:**

* Implement a Warehouse Management System (WMS) to streamline inventory management and optimize workflows
* Use Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) for predictive maintenance and real-time monitoring
* Invest in robotics and automation to increase efficiency and reduce labor costs
