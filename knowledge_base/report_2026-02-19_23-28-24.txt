**Industry Best Practices:**

* **Optimize Warehouse Layout:** According to Prologis, optimizing warehouse layout can reduce travel distances and improve picking efficiency (Prologis, 2020).
* **Implement Zone Picking:** RelayPro suggests implementing zone picking to reduce travel distances and improve picking times (RelayPro, 2020).
* **Use Data Analytics:** Cybernetman emphasizes the importance of using data analytics to monitor and improve warehouse operations (Cybernetman, 2020).

**Recommendations:**

1. **Implement Zone Picking:** Divide the warehouse into zones and assign specific products to each zone to reduce travel distances and improve picking efficiency.
2. **Optimize Warehouse Layout:** Analyze the current warehouse layout and make adjustments to reduce travel distances and improve picking efficiency.
3. **Monitor and Analyze Picking Times:** Use data analytics to monitor and analyze picking times for different products and transport modes to identify areas for improvement.
4. **Train Warehouse Staff:** Provide training to warehouse staff on efficient picking techniques and zone picking to improve overall warehouse productivity.
