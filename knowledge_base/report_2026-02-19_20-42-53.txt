**Introduction:**

This report aims to analyze warehouse operations using internal warehouse dataset and real industry web research to identify root causes, provide insights, and recommend best practices for improvement.

**Root Cause Analysis:**

The internal warehouse dataset reveals the following key performance indicators (KPIs):

1. **Picking Time Variability:** The picking time for order 1002 (Furniture) is 18 minutes, while order 1005 (Furniture) takes 50 minutes. This suggests that picking time is influenced by product type and warehouse location.
2. **Transport Mode Selection:** The dataset shows that Van is used for both Bhopal and Indore warehouses, despite the longer distance to Indore (300 km). This may indicate inefficient transport mode selection.
3. **Delay Incidence:** Order 1005 (Furniture) experienced a delay, which may be attributed to the longer picking time or inefficient transport mode selection.

**Insights:**

1. **Product Type Influence:** Furniture products tend to have longer picking times compared to Clothing products.
2. **Warehouse Location Impact:** Warehouse location affects picking time, with Indore warehouse experiencing longer picking times.
3. **Transport Mode Optimization:** Inefficient transport mode selection may lead to delays and increased costs.

**Industry Best Practices:**

1. **Optimize Warehouse Layout:** Implement a warehouse layout that minimizes walking distances and maximizes storage capacity (Source: [1])
2. **Implement Zone Picking:** Divide the warehouse into zones to reduce picking time and increase efficiency (Source: [2])
3. **Use Transportation Management Systems (TMS):** Leverage TMS to optimize transport mode selection, routing, and scheduling (Source: [3])
4. **Monitor and Analyze KPIs:** Regularly track and analyze KPIs to identify areas for improvement and optimize warehouse operations (Source: [1])

**Recommendations:**

1. **Implement Zone Picking:** Divide the warehouse into zones to reduce picking time and increase efficiency.
2. **Optimize Warehouse Layout:** Reconfigure the warehouse layout to minimize walking distances and maximize storage capacity.
3. **Implement TMS:** Leverage TMS to optimize transport mode selection, routing, and scheduling.
4. **Monitor and Analyze KPIs:** Regularly track and analyze KPIs to identify areas for improvement and optimize warehouse operations.

**Conclusion:**

This report highlights the importance of analyzing warehouse operations to identify areas for improvement. By implementing industry best practices and recommendations, warehouse operations can be optimized to reduce picking time, increase efficiency, and minimize delays.
