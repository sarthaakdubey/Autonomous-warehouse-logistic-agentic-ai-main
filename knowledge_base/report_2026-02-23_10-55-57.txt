**Industry Best Practices:**

* **Optimize Product Storage:** Store frequently ordered products in easily accessible locations to reduce picking times (Source: DTG Power).
* **Implement Zone Picking:** Divide the warehouse into zones and assign pickers to specific zones to reduce travel time and increase efficiency (Source: Cybernet Man).
* **Use RFID Technology:** Implement RFID tracking to improve inventory accuracy and reduce delays (Source: RFID Discovery).
* **Analyze Transport Mode Selection:** Regularly review transport mode selection to ensure optimal routes and reduce costs (Source: Industry Web Research).
