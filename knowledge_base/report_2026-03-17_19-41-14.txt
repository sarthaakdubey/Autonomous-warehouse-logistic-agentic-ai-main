**Warehouse Automation Technologies Report**

**1. Root Cause:**
* Delays in warehouse operations are primarily caused by inefficient warehouse operations, slower picking times, and distance and transport mode.

**2. Insights:**
* Larger quantities do not necessarily cause delays, but rather inefficient warehouse operations.
* The Indore warehouse has slower picking times, contributing to its higher delay rate.
* Implementing warehouse automation technologies can help streamline operations, improve inventory accuracy, and reduce labor costs.

**3. Industry Best Practices:**
* Implement a Warehouse Management System (WMS) to streamline operations and improve inventory accuracy.
* Use Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) to optimize material movement and reduce labor costs.
* Leverage Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to predict demand, optimize inventory levels, and improve supply chain visibility.
* Implement a Zone Picking system to reduce travel time and increase picking efficiency.
* Use Dimensioning and Weighing systems to improve inventory accuracy and reduce errors.
* Implement a Dynamic Slotting system to optimize storage and retrieval of inventory.
